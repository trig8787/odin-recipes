Taco recipe

Description

This is a simple taco recipe. These tacos will be ready within 15 minutes and are an absolute delight for your taste buds.

Ingredients

Filling

  • 1 lb ground beef or 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1/2 white onion, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 3/4 cup water or 3/4 cup beer
  • 8 fresh cilantro stems, minced
  • 1 pinch sugar

Toppings

  • lettuce, shredded
  • cheese, shredded
  • tomatos, seeded and chopped
  • sour cream
  • sliced jalapeno
  • corn
  • beans
  • rice
  • avocado

Steps

  1. Cook meat, onion, garlic, and cilantro, stirring often until meat is no longer pink
  2. Drain fat
  3. Add seasoning and 3/4 cup water or beer to meat mixture and stir to blend
  4. Simmer until liquid has evaporated
  5. Serve with desired toppings
